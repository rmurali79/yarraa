About Yarraa

Yarraa is all about providing an easy tracking and hassle-free Online Reverse Logistics Solution for consumers, retailers, distributors, service centers, and manufacturers. Our name is derived from the Wurundjeri term Yarra Yarra, meaning 'flow'; and the Yarra River, a waterway famed for being the only river that flows 'in reverse'. Putting the two elements together, the idea is a perfect fit to Yarraa's business: that of streamlined reverse logistics.
