Information Collection and Use

YARRAA is the sole owner of the information collected on this site. We will not sell, share, or rent this information to any outside parties, except as outlined in this policy. YARRAA collects information from our users at several different points on our website in order to process orders and better serve you with pertinent information. Information collected includes name, shipping address, billing address, telephone number, e-mail address, and payment information such as a credit card number. YARRAA also requires you to submit a user name and password of your choice for future access to your information. Your user name and password are to remain confidential and you should not share this information with anyone.

Registration

In order to process your orders using this website, you must first complete the registration form. During registration a user is required to give his or her contact information, which includes name, e-mail address, telephone number and postal address. This information is used to contact the user about the services on our site, such as sending automated order status updates.

Order

Most of the information collected in the registration process will be used to process orders. In the order process, a user must provide additional financial information such as credit card number, expiration date, money order, wire transfer or check information. This information is used for billing purposes and to fulfil customers' orders. If we have trouble processing an order, this contact information is used to get in touch with the user. We do not share your personal and financial information with any third parties. The only exception is when processing your credit card information with the merchant bank for authorization and approval. This process is protected by an enhanced security system. See Security paragraph for more details on security measures and procedures.

Cookies

We use cookies to customize Web page content based on visitors' browser type or other information that the visitor sends. If you reject the cookie, you may still use our site; however, you will be unable to use our shopping cart to purchase items online. This is because cookies are used by the shopping cart to retain information necessary to process your order. If you disable cookies, we will not be able to accept your order online. We do not share any information with any third party sites. Cookies are used strictly for the purpose of enhancing your personal experience with our site. When you close your browser, the cookies will be terminated.

Log Files

We use IP addresses to analyze trends, administer the site, track users' movement, and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use. IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information. YARRAA will record demographic information such as the IP addresses and postal codes of clients. This information will not be shared without express permission from clients. We do not distribute or share IP information with any third parties. YARRAA will not disclose clients' personal information to any outside party, and will practice discretion to the greatest extent possible within its own offices. YARRAA employees will have access to clients' personal information only in the event that they need to see it. Clients accept responsibility for practicing similar discretion with their passwords. Clients' financial information (credit card numbers, etc.) will not be stored, in order to ensure clients' security.

Sharing

We use a credit card processing company to bill users for our services. This company does not retain, share, store or use personally identifiable information for any secondary purposes. YARRAA is a California Corporation and shall abide by all federal and state laws. YARRAA shall disclose any personally identifiable information when required to by law.

Links

This web site contains links to other sites. Please be aware YARRAA is not responsible for the privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of each and every web site that collects personally identifiable information. This privacy statement applies solely to information collected by this Web site.

Rating Products and Reviews

YARRAA provides a product-rating feature that allows customers to rate the quality of the product. Our review system also allows for customers to leave written comments to further elaborate their experience with the product. All ratings and review comments are pre-screened by a moderator before posting to the site. The moderator may choose to disregard or eliminate comments that do not meet the guidelines. Guidelines include that all comments are truthful and non-offensive. Guidelines do not permit the posting of personal information or information for the purpose of personal gain.

Security

YARRAA takes every precaution to protect our users' information. When users submit sensitive information via the website, your information is protected both online and off-line. When our registration/order form asks users to enter sensitive information (such as a credit card number), that information is encrypted, and is protected with the best encryption software in the industry - SSL. Our SSL technology is the most advanced 128bit encryption by Authorize.net. To learn more about SSL, please visit www.Authorize.net. All YARRAA employees are kept up-to-date on our security and privacy practices. Every quarter, as well as any time new policies are added, our employees are notified and/or reminded about the importance we place on privacy, and what they can do to ensure our customers information is protected. Additionally, YARRAA subscribes to the practice of responsible disclosure. In the event of vulnerable information appearing online, removal of said information will take highest priority, and the breach will not be acknowledged until it has been repaired. If you have any questions about the security at our website, you can send an email to us at feedback@yarraa.com.

Special Offers

If you choose to receive special offers, YARRAA may send you pertinent information. Our special offers are sent only to customers who grant YARRAA permission to send such information. YARRAA does not share your information with third parties and does not solicit outside parties to send special offers on our behalf. Out of respect for the privacy of our users we present the option to not receive these types of communications. At the time of your order, you may opt to not receive special offers and news by un-checking the appropriate field. Furthermore, all special offers are sent via e-mail and have a "remove link" option should you wish to no longer receive special offers and news. Otherwise, customers may occasionally receive information on products, services, special deals or news. Please see our choice and opt-out below for further details of how to be removed from our mailing list.

Choice/Opt-out

YARRAA does not use customers' information for contact other than YARRAA pertinent business. YARRAA does not share our customers' personal information with any outside parties. However, for customers who wish to opt-out or be removed from YARRAA special offers and related news, YARRAA provides several methods to accomplish this request. Users who no longer wish to receive our newsletter or promotional materials may opt-out of receiving these communications by un-checking the permission to send field when processing their order. They may also send email to remove@yarraa.com with a request to be removed. Finally, they may also use the link provided with all communications to automatically unsubscribe. Clients will have the right to disclose product information directly to partners with a need to know said information; YARRAA and other third parties will not have access.

Notification of Changes

If at any point we decide to use personally identifiable information in a manner different from that stated at the time it was collected, we will notify users by way of an email. Users will have a choice as to whether or not we use their information in this different manner. We will use information in accordance with the privacy policy under which the information was collected. If you have opted out of receiving any communications from YARRAA, you may visit our website at www.yarraa.com where news of changes will be posted. Otherwise, you may e-mail us at feedback@yarraa.com and ask our customer service department for changes and updates. If you send us an e-mail request, we will respond to that e-mail only. You will not receive promotional materials unless you specifically grant us permission to do so.

User Responsibilities

By using YARRAA services, account-holders agree to the following terms.

Manufacturers

Users who hold manufacturer accounts agree to register only products they make. Any manufacturers who are found to be registering products they do not produce will be blocked from further access to YARRAA, and will not receive a refund on any remaining time in their paid subscriptions.

Distributors

Users who hold distributor accounts agree to register only products they distribute (i.e. products they either receive directly from a manufacturer or provide directly to a retailer). Distributors who are found to be registering products they do not handle will be blocked from further access to YARRAA, and will not receive a refund on any remaining time in their paid subscriptions.

Service Centres

Users who hold service centre accounts agree to register only cases in which they were directly involved in repairs. Service centre users also agree to register only product brands and models with which they are licensed to work. Any service centre accounts that are found to contain false authorization information (i.e. claiming to be authorized for any brand or product for which the centre is not really authorized) will be blocked from further access to YARRAA, and will not receive a refund on any remaining time in their paid subscriptions. In the event that an authorized service centre is found to be listed as unauthorized (in order to save money on subscriptions or for any other reason), the centre's listing will be changed immediately to reflect its true nature and its YARRAA account will be charged to reflect the correct status. YARRAA also reserves the right to terminate the centre's account in such an instance.

Retailers

Users who hold retailer accounts agree to register only brands they sell. As with manufacturers, failure to do so will result in immediate termination of the user's YARRAA account. Retailers also agree not to engage in unsolicited mass e-mails to consumers with contact information obtained through YARRAA. Abuse of this rule may be subject to deletion of retailers' accounts.

Consumers

Users who hold consumer accounts agree to register only products that belong to them. Any registrations of products under a user's name that do not belong to that user, or uploading of otherwise fraudulent documents, will be grounds for immediate cancellation of the user's YARRAA account. Users understand and accept that YARRAA can be used to manage all their products, but not necessarily all brands. If a consumer purchases a product made or sold by a company that does not use YARRAA, the consumer may use YARRAA to keep track of warranty information, purchase records and the like; but services like warranty extensions and locations of service centres will not be available until and unless the company joins YARRAA. YARRAA is not responsible for any policy changes made by other companies that affect consumers' use of YARRAA services. All YARRAA ads are based on genuine consumer feedback; consumers therefore accept that their feedback may be used as representative of users' experience with YARRAA.

All Categories

All users of YARRAA accept responsibility for uploading product records and other information only if said information is factually correct to the best of their knowledge. Knowingly uploading false information is grounds for immediate termination of any YARRAA account, including paid accounts.
