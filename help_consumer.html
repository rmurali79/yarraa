How can I update my personal information?

To update any personal information (address, telephone number, etc.), click on My Account. This will lead you to a screen where you can add and/or change your personal information:

Only your First Name, Last Name (Family Name), Country and ZIP Code (or Postal Code) are required. The Image Upload is simply a personalization option: you may upload an image to appear in the far upper right hand corner of your screen whenever you log on to YARRAA. The Secret Question and Answer are an added security measure; if you fill in those fields, you will be prompted with the security question and required to enter the answer when you log in. If you do not wish to add that layer of security, simply leave those fields blank.

When you are done adding or changing information, click on Update and the screen will refresh with your updated information. (If there is any problem with the new information, red text will appear next to the field where the problem occurred, explaining what's wrong and how you can fix it.)

How do I change my password?

1. Click on Change My Password in the upper left hand corner of your screen. You will be directed to this page:

Enter the old password and the New Password as directed. To avoid accidentally entering a misspelled word, you must enter the new password a second time in the Confirm Password field.

2. Click Submit. Provided the new password matches in the second and third field, you will then see the above screen again, with the fields empty and with "Password Successfully Changed" across the top of the screen.

Why must I include my Postal Code/ZIP Code in my account information?

Convenience is an important element of YARRAA's services. We want to provide you with the best options for getting the fastest and most dependable service for all your household products. In order to achieve this goal, we need to know where you are so that we can provide you with the nearest service centers. We do this using Google Maps technology, which identifies locations in most parts of the world through their postal codes or ZIP codes. Without this information, we may not be able to identify a service center that meets your needs and is located acceptably close to you.

Please rest assured that, as with all personal information, YARRAA will never make your postal/ZIP code available to any third party without your express permission. Our own staff will not have access to your address except in cases where it is directly relevant to serving your needs.

How do I add a product?

1. Go to Consumers Home, then Add New Product. You should then see a field labeled "Register Your Product Here" on the right-hand side of the screen:

2. Both the Serial No. and the Brand are required. The serial number can almost always be found somewhere on the package or on the item itself. (A UPC number or IMEI number is acceptable in lieu of the serial number.)

3. After you click Next, the following screen will appear:

All fields marked with an asterisk are required. Although the others are not required, the more information you supply, the better YARRAA will be able to serve you. The Model name and/or number will almost always be found on the package, and often on the item itself. The Warranty Number and Bill No. can usually be found on your receipt. You may also save copies of your receipt, invoice and warranty policy as Attachments.

4. Click Submit. You should then see all the information you entered immediately:

If any of the information you enter needs to be updated at a later date, simply click on the Edit Product tab and repeat the above steps.

How do I delete a product?

To delete a product, simply locate it on your My Products List, and click on the check-box next to it:

Click on Delete. You will then be asked if you are sure you wish to delete the product. Click Yes. Your screen should then revert to the My Products List, without the deleted product.

How do I find a product in my products list?

You can search through your products list by Brand, Product Type, Serial Number, or Model. To do this, go to the Search function at the upper left hand corner of the Consumer Home screen, and choose the criterion by which you want to search. Then, enter the brand, model, type or number you wish to search for in the field. Click Search. Your My Products List will then show all registered items that fit the terms of your search.

How do I edit the product information for a product already in my list?

Choose the product from your My Products List. The Product Information field will then show the existing information for that product. Click on the Edit Product tab. You can then change, add or delete any information you like except for the brand name, model, serial number, product, or the warranty dates. After making the desired changes, click Update at the bottom of the field. The changes should then appear.

How can I track my warranty expiration dates?

Yarraa does this for you with all your registered items. A list of your warranties, and the number of days left for each of them, is found under the Warranty Alert heading on your Consumer Home screen. If you are concerned about impending expiration dates, you can sort your items by clicking on the arrow icon next to the Warranty Remaining Days heading. You can then choose to show items expiring in one year, one month, or expired already. Note that the list will then show only those items; to see all your warranties again, you must choose Show All.

How do I create a service request?

1. On your My Products List, click on the name of the product for which you want to create a service request. The Product Information for that item will then appear on the right hand side of your screen.

2. Click on the Service Request tab. You will then see the following:

All fields marked by an asterisk are required. Once you select your Country of residence, the page will reload; you will then have a list of authorized service centers in your country to choose from. Authorized Service Centers is required field; however, if you prefer to use an other service center, you may choose "Other" from the list of authorized service centers. (Note that Failure Desc is a required field; this will enable the service center of your choice to assist you much more efficiently.) When all required information is entered, click Submit.

3. The information you entered will be forwarded immediately to the service center of your choice. You can then bring your product to the service center, mail it there, or arrange to have it picked up in arrangement with each center's policy. (The service center may contact you directly with further instructions.) You can print out a copy of the service request for your records and/or to show to the service center staff. After you submit the request, the item will be listed under My Service Requests, at the bottom of the Consumers Home page. The Service Status field tells you whether the product is still being worked on (Open) and will serve as notification when it has been fixed.

How do I locate a service center?

1. The first step is to begin the process of creating a service request. After you enter the country in which you wish to find a service center, the page should refresh automatically.

2. Under Service Centers Location at the bottom right hand corner of your screen, you will find a list of authorized service centers for the product you chose in Step 1:

Note that authorized service centers are denoted with the blue icon, other service centers are denoted with the red icon, and registered Yarraa users are denoted with the icon. While other service centers are often less expensive, some warranties are valid only for service at authorized centers. To locate each center, click on the name. A local map will then appear with the location of the service center highlighted.

What is a Quotation?

For some products, you can get an estimate on the price of services before submitting a service request. This is called a quotation.

To get a quotation, click on the product you're interested in servicing in your My Products List. The product details will then appear under Product Information on the right hand side of the screen. Click on the Quotation tab.

If quotation services are available for that company, you will be directed to a screen that will prompt you to identify the desired service and choose a service center. When you click Submit, the service center will receive your request for a quotation, and will reply shortly through YARRAA with a price estimate for its services. You will be notified of the response and presented with an offer on your Product Information screen. To accept the offer and initiate the service request, click Approve. If the price is not acceptable, click Regret.

If quotation services are not available, the message "Quotation not applicable" will appear on your Product Information screen.

How do I revoke a service request?

A service request can only be revoked from the account that created it. If you filed a service request through a retailer, only the retailer can revoke it.

To revoke a service request you filed directly through YARRAA, go to the My Service Requests list at the bottom of the Consumers Home page. Click on the name of the product for which you wish to cancel the service request. The service request will then appear in the upper right hand corner of the screen:

To cancel the request, click Revoke. If you only wish to edit the request, you may make any changes you wish at this point and then click Update. Note that the Revoke and Update buttons will only appear in cases where you have the authority to change or cancel the request (i.e. requests you initiated directly from your YARRAA account). Any changes or cancellations you make will be submitted directly to the service center; you need take no further action.

I want the Service Centers Location map to show another locality. How do I do this?

The map defaults to your current location, regardless of whether you live or work there. If you want to search for service centers in another location, simply choose a search criterion (City, Country, ZIP Code) on the Search By icon, and then enter the city, country or code in the field next to it. A new map will then appear showing the location you have requested.

How can I track the progress of my service requests?

All open requests are listed in the My Service Requests field, at the lower left-hand corner of the screen. The service status column indicates the status of each request: Requested (the request has been received by the service center, but no action has been taken yet); In Progress (repairs have begun); Ready for Collection (repairs are complete).
