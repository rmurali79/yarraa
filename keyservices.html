Consumers

You also earn Yarraa Points every time you use Yarraa, and you can exchange them for valuable rewards and discounts on your future visits. Next time you need any information regarding your purchases after you leave the store, find it all here!

Retailers

If you carry many different brands and items, chances are you have a wide variety of different post sales issues and returns policies to manage. But with Yarraa, you can manage all your post-sales issues, for all your consumers and all your brands – in one place! Whether it's your consumers or your suppliers who have questions about a product, you can get fast and accurate answers through Yarraa.

 
 

Repairs? Warranties? Yarraa has the answers – and more!

  • Find out where an individual product has been, where it is now, and what services have been performed on it.
  • Register warranties for your consumers' purchases in a matter of seconds – no more warranty cards to copy or lose!
  • Earn commissions when consumers purchase warranty extensions through Yarraa.
  • If your store acts as a collection point, you can route products to and from service centres, and submit service requests on behalf of consumers.

Manage all your inventory and product return needs in one place

  • Keep track of your inventory and know at a glance which stocks are getting low.
  • Create reports of stocks, sales and services for any of your products and brands.
  • All your staffers can update all open cases with Yarraa, so everybody can stay informed.

With all this information from one source, it's easy to improve customer service and create stronger relationships with your customers.

Services centers

Reaching consumers in need of your services has never been easier. They can find and contact you directly through Yarraa, and you will automatically be provided with all the purchase- and warranty-information you need.

 

Find consumers, offer price quotations, earn commissions – it's easy with Yarraa!

  • Provide price estimates for consumers to approve before you commence repairs.
  • Get in touch with consumers to offer troubleshooting advice that will save time and resources on unnecessary repair services.
  • Earn commissions when your clients purchase our shipping services to send their items to you.

Manage your technicians, supplies and open cases with Yarraa

  • Create user accounts for your technicians at no extra cost. This enables you to track your technicians' performance and workloads, so when items arrive for repairs you can route them immediately to the staffer best able to perform the repair.
  • Have your technicians keep track of the replacement parts they use in real time, so you always know the supply levels for all your parts.
  • Purchase shipping services from UPS at reduced rates.

With Yarraa, you can streamline your reverse logistics process. This leads to more efficient - and inexpensive – shipping, as well.

Listed Service Centres

If you don't feel Yarraa's after-purchase services are right for you, you can still use Yarraa to reach out to consumers. As a listed service centre, your contact information will appear on our list of service centres that consumers will see when they search for repair services near them. This service is available for as little as $10 USD per year.

Distributors

Reverse shipments are inefficient by nature, but Yarraa can help you streamline all your shipments in all directions.

 

More efficient shipping saves you money and resources!

  • Through our routing system, you know what shipments are on the way – and from whom – even before your partners send them.
  • Keep track of the current location and status of every item your distribution centre has handled, as well as your current inventory and stock balances.
  • Since Yarraa is universal, you can manage all your retail networks and suppliers in one place, no matter how many different companies are involved. Yarraa keeps you in touch with them all, through the same portal.

Optimize your storage space with Yarraa

  • Arrange accordingly for storage and/or onward shipments, and you can consolidate various packages with the same destination to maximize your shipping efficiency.
  • The improved turnaround time from Yarraa enables you to optimize your use of storage space, and accept more shipments, thus increasing your client base and your revenue.

Manufacturers

Yarraa offers an unprecedented level of consumer relationship management (CRM). Now you can keep up to date on all post sales issues for all your products, without waiting for feedback from retailers or service centres!

 

Take care of your products even after they've been sold!

  • Yarraa provides all the information you need on the post-manufacture histories of all your products: where they are now, what if anything has gone wrong with them, and the corrective action taken.
  • Detect and eliminate design- and manufacturing-flaws more quickly than ever before, and without depending on anybody else to tell you what's wrong. With Yarraa, you can spot rising failure rates and take steps to get them under control immediately.
  • Our instant warranty-registration system also cuts down on the risk of fraudulent returns, so you can guard against fakes and still meet honest customers' after-purchase needs.

Improve consumer relations with Yarraa

  • Get in touch directly with all your end-users via e-mail or SMS, no matter where in the world they are.
  • Earn commissions when our consumers purchase our shipping, insurance and warranty extension services for your products.

And since Yarraa is universal, you can do all this via the same account for all the retailers and distributors you work with!

Recyclers

Manage recycling needs for everyone, from anywhere, in one place.

 

Find and communicate with consumers in your area through Yarraa

  • When consumers in your area submit a recycling request, Yarraa automatically forwards it directly to you.
  • You can then get in touch with the consumer through Yarraa with a price offer, and arrange the delivery on your own terms.
  • Earn commissions when consumers use our UPS link to purchase shipping services to your facilities.
  • Yarraa shows at a glance which offers have been accepted and which are outstanding, so you always know the exact status of each transaction.

Manage business relations with Yarraa

  • You can partner with suppliers and distributors through Yarraa, for responsible product disposal of all their products.
  • All products, all brands - no matter how many different types of recycling you handle, you can handle them all through Yarraa.

And since Yarraa is universal, you can do all this via the same account for all the retailers and distributors you work with!
